Style

Your personal style is a reflection of who you are, so choosing colors, outfits and accessories that match your personality can boost confidence and make a lasting impression.

Finding your undertone helps you choose clothing, makeup and accessories that naturally complement your overall look and glow.

Cool Undertone

Best Colors Emerald, Lavender, Royal Blue, Silver, Ruby Red
Style Tips Choose silver jewelry. Stick to jewel tones to brighten your skin.
Avoid Orange, Mustard Yellow and Warm Golds.

Warm Undertone

Best Colors Honey Gold, Olive Green, Peach, Cream, Terracotta
Style Tips Gold jewelry is your best friend. Earthy tones make you glow.
Avoid Icy Blues and Neon Pinks.

Neutral Undertone

Best Colors Dusty Rose, Jade Green, Navy, Off-White, Soft Grey
Style Tips You can wear both Gold and Silver! Muted tones look best.
Avoid Anything too neon that might overwhelm your balance.
