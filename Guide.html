Get To Know Some Skin Anatomy !
Epidermis :

the outermost layer of the skin and the part we see , Contains melanin that gives skin its color.

What does it do ?

Controls water loss to keep the skin hydrated, protect skin from uv rays and bacteria

Dermis

The dermis lies beneath the epidermis and is the thickest layer of the skin.

What does it do ?

Provides strength and flexibility, help skin heal and repair.

Hypodermis

Also called Subcutaneous Layer, the deepest layer of the skin, made mainly of fat.

What does it do ?

Connects the skin to muscles and bone, Insulates the body and helps regulate temperature, Stores energy in the form of fat.

skin layers are the same for every human being, but skin type (the first layer specificaly) differs from individual to other

common skin types are 5:

Normal skin

Oily skin

Combinational Skin

Dry Skin

Sensitive Skin



Dry Skin

Causes:

Eczema, genetics , enviroment , excissive scrubing

Management:

Regular and proper hydration, gentle shaving

Avoid:

extreme hot bath, extreme scubbing



Oily Skin

Causes:

Hormonal changes, genetics, humid climate

Management:

Use oil-free products, apply light-weight makeup, use water-based moistrizers

Avoid:

Heavy creams, alcohol-based products, overwashing



Normal Skin

no fine lines? redness ? excissive oils ? you are lucky !

Management:

make sure you use products with balanced texture & oils amount (not too oily, not too light either)

Avoid:

extreme hot-cold water, skipping sunscreen



Sensitive Skin

Causes:

weak skin barries, allergies to some ingredients , enviromental triggers

Management:

Apply sunscreen regularly, fragrance-free and hypoallergenic product usage

Avoid:

Frequent products switching, acid based products



Combinational Skin

Causes:

Uneven oil production (oily T-zone, dry cheeks), Using unsuitable skincare products

Management:

Apply light moisturizer on oily areas and richer cream on dry areas, Use gentle, balanced cleansers

Avoid:

Using products designed only for very oily or very dry skin, Skipping moisturizer
